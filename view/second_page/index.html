About your host, Luis, Ernesto & Sonia

We are three friends—a programmer, an architect, and an art historian—who share the same passion of riding bicycles throughout Havana with our friends.

What we’ll do

We'll pick up the bikes and ride along our favorite streets of Havana's central Vedado neighborhood. Before crossing the Almendares River by the Iron Bridge, we will stop to taste a variety of delicious natural juices. Then we will visit the Havana Forest along its trails, where you will enjoy a natural and incredible atmosphere to take photos.

What I’ll provide

Water & Natural Juice

One small bottle of water and up to three natural juices are included.

Entry to Colon Cemetery

Entrance fee of 5 dollars is included.

Bike, helmet, and lock

Who can come

Guests ages 12 and up can attend.

Notes

Wear long sleeves and comfortable shoes. We highly recommend that you bring sunscreen and sunglasses for protection. Also don't forget your camera, because this is an amazing ride for taking photos.

Where we’ll be

We will make our way through the central neighborhood of Vedado. Along the way you will see the Malecon Habanero—the broad coastal road. Then we will cross the Almendares River and see the Havana Forest, the Colon Cemetery, Revolution Square, and Havana University.
